     6вҶ’     6вҶ’## 0. Philosophy: The "Swiss Vault" Model
     7вҶ’     7вҶ’
     8вҶ’     8вҶ’Every user is a **record** in one system. Not "members" and "non-members" вҖ” one continuous spectrum from anonymous visitor вҶ’ registered user вҶ’ token holder вҶ’ paying subscriber вҶ’ enterprise client. The admin sees everyone, controls everything, and can grant or revoke access with one click.
     9вҶ’     9вҶ’
    10вҶ’    10вҶ’Content is **SRC intellectual property** at all times. Every share, every forward, every download carries the SRC brand, copyright notice, and a link back. Think Financial Times "ft.com" on every paragraph. Think The Economist's share links that always route through their domain.

   519вҶ’   519вҶ’- **Cancel**: Immediate or at period end. Churn survey shown to user.
   520вҶ’   520вҶ’- **Refund**: Partial or full via Stripe. `subscription_events` row with `event_type='refunded'`.
   521вҶ’   521вҶ’- **Impersonate**: Login as user to see exactly what they see. Every action logged in `impersonation_logs`.
   522вҶ’   522вҶ’- **Schedule Change**: Plan upgrade/downgrade/cancel for future date (e.g., "downgrade to Professional at next renewal")
