Screen 4 — Gating Logic: Full-access users see the complete article with PDF download, share, and comment actions. Teaser users see ~40% of the content (soft gate), then a paywall overlay with tier-specific upgrade CTA. The paywall shows social proof ("200+ exclusive studies") relevant to the target tier, not generic messaging.

Screen 6 — Admin CRM: Central hub for all member management. Stats row shows total members, active tokens (with expiry warnings), and MRR. Member table filters by tier, status, engagement score. Color-coded engagement bars (green 70-100, yellow 30-69, red 0-29). Right sidebar: task board with overdue alerts, token inventory with caps (300 total), quick actions, and referral leaderboard. "Nudge" action sends re-engagement email to dormant token holders. "Win-back" triggers churn recovery campaign.
