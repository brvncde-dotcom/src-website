## 2. The 4-Tier Membership Architecture (~3000 words, 4 tables)
### 2.1 Tier Philosophy: The "Access Ladder"
#### 2.1.1 "Open by default" norm: Brookings, Carnegie, ECFR all make core research free — paid tiers differentiate through experience, not basic content
#### 2.1.2 Naming strategy: avoid "Basic" (signals inadequacy); use value-based names: Observer → Essential → Professional → Executive — converts 15-30% better
#### 2.1.3 The "Good-Better-Best" ratio: Basic→Premium = 2.7x; Premium→Expert = 1.9x — both exceed minimum 50% gap requirement
### 2.2 Free Tier: "Observer"
#### 2.2.1 Registration wall after 2-3 articles (16x more effective than newsletter forms); registered users convert at 10x rate of anonymous visitors
#### 2.2.2 Content: opinions, short briefs (<1,000 words), selected policy briefs (executive summary), free newsletter, podcasts, infographics
#### 2.2.3 Limits: 5-10 selected pieces/month + unlimited opinions/briefs; 6-month rolling archive; full catalog visible for discovery
#### 2.2.4 Purpose: habit formation, SEO, social sharing, funnel entry — the "aha moment" occurs when users reference SRC research in their work
### 2.3 Essential Tier (Basic Replacement)
#### 2.3.1 CHF 29/month (CHF 290/year) — positions between RANE $32/mo and Geopolitical Desk £29/mo
#### 2.3.2 Unlimited access to all reports (2,000-4,000 words with methodology, data annexes, source citations)
#### 2.3.3 PDF downloads, 12-month archive, ad-free experience, daily brief newsletter
#### 2.3.4 Monthly virtual briefing (recorded), full video access (not clips), read-only community access
### 2.4 Professional Tier (Premium)
#### 2.4.1 CHF 79/month (CHF 790/year) — target tier with "Most Popular" badge, 2.7x price jump justified by feature differentiation
#### 2.4.2 Exclusive studies (3,000-8,000 words), weekly deep-dive, quarterly strategic forecast, annual flagship Strategic Outlook
#### 2.4.3 Full vAvatar video library: 90-120 second daily briefs, 7-10 minute weekly analysis, 15-30 minute expert interviews
#### 2.4.4 Early access: 24-48 hours before lower tiers; premium-only newsletters; interactive scenario tools; 3-seat team access
### 2.5 Executive Tier (Expert)
#### 2.5.1 CHF 149/month (CHF 1,490/year) — anchor tier with relationship pricing; annual billing only
#### 2.5.2 Live analyst access: monthly group video call (Chatham House Rule), quarterly 1:1 consultation (30 min), flash brief within 4 hours
#### 2.5.3 Real-time breaking news alerts (in-app push + SMS + email), customizable by topic/region
#### 2.5.4 Annual "SRC Global Security Summit" in Switzerland — invitation-only, 2-day event with private briefings
#### 2.5.5 Bespoke research requests (4/year), dedicated account manager, priority access to new features

## 3. Content Gating & Delivery Architecture (~2500 words, 3 tables)
### 3.1 Content Type Taxonomy
#### 3.1.1 Opinions (500-800 words): always free — quick takes, reactions, commentary — awareness and SEO building
#### 3.1.2 Reports (2,000-4,000 words): executive summary free, full report Essential+ — structured analysis with data
#### 3.1.3 Studies (3,000-8,000 words): Professional+ exclusive — deep-dive research with proprietary methodology, scenario analysis
#### 3.1.4 Briefings: flash alert (Expert), daily brief (Essential+), weekly roundup (all tiers) — tiered by speed and depth
#### 3.1.5 Video: opinions clips free, vAvatar briefings Essential+, full interviews Professional+, live sessions Executive
#### 3.1.6 Interviews: transcript excerpts free, full transcripts Essential+, video Professional+, live Q&A Executive
### 3.2 The Reverse Timewall Content Cascade
#### 3.2.1 Content flows DOWN through tiers over time: Expert (immediate) → Professional (+24h) → Essential (+72h) → Free (+2 weeks)
#### 3.2.2 BoiseDev 24h timewall model generates revenue without locking public out; Mittmedia reverse paywall achieves 20% conversion
#### 3.2.3 Breaking news: Expert gets flash alert within 4 hours, Professional gets same-day analysis, Essential gets next-day brief, Free gets weekly digest
### 3.3 vAvatar Video Section Strategy
#### 3.3.1 Hybrid model: AI avatars deliver 80% of video content (daily/weekly briefings), human experts for 20% (premium interviews, crisis content)
#### 3.3.2 Technology stack: Synthesia for European languages (4.1/5 lip-sync DE/FR), HeyGen for social content — production cost $0.59-$2.50/minute vs $1,000-$10,000+ traditional
#### 3.3.3 Trust framework: "AI-delivered, expert-curated" framing with full pipeline transparency; EU AI Act editorial exemption for expert-reviewed content
#### 3.3.4 Multilingual delivery: same briefing in DE/FR/IT/EN simultaneously — 4x audience reach at marginal cost
#### 3.3.5 Video types by tier: Free (90-sec clips YouTube) → Essential (daily 2-min briefing) → Professional (weekly 10-min analysis + interviews) → Executive (live sessions + on-demand 1:1)
